## Project Overview
This project analyzes transactional data from SuperStore, a retail company selling office supplies, technology, and furniture. The goal is to provide actionable business recommendations through exploratory data analysis and interactive visualizations.

**Key Objectives**:
- Identify profitable and loss-making products
- Segment customers using RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
- Optimize shipping modes 

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ibrahimelsayed182/superstore
- Rows: 9,994 transactions
- Columns: 21

## Business Recommendations
- Optimize production & marketing for Paper, Accessories, and Furnishings
- Reduce discounts on low-margin products like Machines (>25% average discount)
- Target promotions to "Loyalist" and "Needs Attention" customers
- Limit premium shipping (First/Second/Same Day) to high-value orders
